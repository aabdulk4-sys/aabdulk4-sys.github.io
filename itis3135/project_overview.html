Web Application Requirements & Design Document

Project Name

Street Kuality – a car-enthusiast community web app focused on automotive photography, event promotion, and collaboration between local creatives and brands.

Project Overview

Function / Purpose: The Street Kuality web app will serve as a digital hub for automotive enthusiasts, photographers, and local events. Users can browse upcoming meets, view car-photography galleries, and connect with other creatives in Charlotte’s automotive community.

Intended Users: Automotive photographers, car enthusiasts, event organizers, and small businesses (car wraps, detailing, etc.) looking to collaborate or promote.

Overview of Content: The site will include a home page, event calendar, photo gallery, “About Us” page, contact form, and submission form for new events or photos.

Site Map

Page Designs

  1. Home Page (index.html)
    • Purpose: Introduce the brand and show featured events or photos.
    • Audience: General visitors.
    • Content: Hero image, tagline, navigation bar, highlight section.
    • Actions: Click to view events or gallery.
  2. Events Page (events.html)
    • Purpose: List upcoming car meets with dates and locations.
    • Content: Interactive table or cards of events with Google Map embeds.
    • Actions: Filter by location/date, RSVP buttons (read-only).
  3. Gallery Page (gallery.html)
    • Purpose: Showcase car photography.
    • Content: Grid of photos with hover effects and lightbox view.
    • Interactivity: jQuery image slider or lightbox plugin.
  4. About Page (about.html)
    • Purpose: Share story of Street Kuality and its founder (Ayah).
    • Content: Biography, brand mission, team image gallery.
  5. Contact Page (contact.html)
    • Purpose: Allow users to submit inquiries or event ideas.
    • Content: Contact form with fields: Name, Email, Message.
    • Validation: JavaScript form validation before submission.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Interactive Gallery: Users can click to enlarge photos (via jQuery Lightbox).
  • Event Filter: Drop-down filter for meet categories using AJAX.
  • Form Validation: JavaScript ensures all required fields are filled correctly.
  • Animated Navbar: Smooth jQuery scroll animations and fade effects.
