Website Evaluations

Apple

The Apple homepage is clean, modern, and visually appealing with clear product emphasis.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of bold typography, white space, and product images to highlight key content.
  • Repetition: Consistent font style, color palette, and navigation across all pages.
  • Alignment: Everything is grid-based and neatly aligned, making the site feel professional and easy to scan.
  • Proximity: Related elements (like product image + description + buy button) are grouped closely together.
