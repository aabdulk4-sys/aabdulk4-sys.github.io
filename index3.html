I'm Ayah Abdulkareem — a UX/UI and Product Designer and recent graduate of UNC Charlotte with a B.A. in Computer Science and a concentration in Human-Centered Design.

How I Work

My Design Process

01
Research & Define
Heuristic evaluation, competitive analysis, and stakeholder interviews to define who is affected and exactly what is broken.
02
Sketch & Wireframe
Low-fidelity sketches to explore directions fast, then digital wireframes in Figma to test structure before any visual polish.
03
Prototype & Test
Interactive prototypes tested with real users through task-based sessions. I observe, document, and iterate — not just validate.
04
Build & Deliver
Final designs implemented in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — ensuring the live product matches the intended experience exactly.
